Akan Name Generator

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana and most parts of West Africa, children are given their first name as a result of 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. To get your corresponding Akan name this web application provides you with a table as well as an automated form. Enjoy!!!
